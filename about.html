Welcome to My Photography Page

I am Riyan Anosh, a future architect of silicon landscapes, currently immersed in the electrifying energy of the Bay Area. My art form isn't confined to a traditional studio; it's the meticulous and imaginative crafting of digital worlds within computer engineering, with a keen aspiration to sculpt the very core of technology through chip design.

What sparks this creative fire? It's the relentless, awe-inspiring evolution of technology itself, that constant drive to push boundaries and invent. This same fascination spills over into the tangible world, manifesting in the exploration of Photoshop's digital canvas, where visual concepts take shape, and in the observant eye that captures the beauty and intricate patterns of the surrounding environment through photography.

Imagine seeing the logical elegance of a circuit diagram imbued with the visual depth of a photograph, the layered complexity of a chip design mirroring the nuanced adjustments within Photoshop. The current focus? Diving deeper into the fundamental principles of chip design, understanding the intricate dance of transistors and logic gates.

Why? Because at the heart of all that captivating technology lies the chip, the very engine that powers the digital age, and Riyan is driven to understand and ultimately contribute to its creation.
